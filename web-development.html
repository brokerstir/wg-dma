Web Development

The web-site is the anchor of your brand or business. Don't leave it to chance.

Ensure its excellence. Seize control while we enhance it.

  • Optimized Content Delivery and User Experience: Provisioning a server is outdated. We leverage Amazon Web Services. Thus, we can tap a globally-distributed network of proxy servers to optimize page loads, ensuring an exceptional user experience marked by seamless speed and reliability.
  • Customization and Architectural Innovation: Our approach emphasizes a higher level of customization and architectural innovation beyond the limitations of template-based solutions. We don't rely on platforms that don't require coding knowledge.
  • Integration of Open Source and Proprietary Framework: We combine open-source code with our proprietary, high-speed development framework to craft tailored solutions that precisely meet our clients' unique needs.

  • Minimalistic and Clean Layouts: Focuses on simplicity, using ample white space, clean typography, and minimal design elements to enhance readability and guide user attention.
  • Mobile-Friendly Design: Start the design process with mobile devices in mind, recognizing the increasing use of smartphones for accessing the internet and prioritizing a great mobile experience.
  • Intuitive Navigation: Designs navigation systems that are intuitive and easy to use, allowing users to find what they need quickly and efficiently.
  • Continuous Iteration and Improvement: An iterative approach to design, collecting feedback, and making data-driven improvements to enhance the website's performance and user satisfaction.

  • Basic Website (Brochure or Landing Page): If you need a simple website with a few pages providing essential information about your business, such as your services, contact details, and a brief description, you can expect to pay anywhere from $1,000 to $3,000. This type of website is suitable for small businesses without need for complexity.
  • Business or Event Website: For a more comprehensive website with additional features like photo galleries, multiple forms, and basic content management, the cost typically ranges from $3,000 to $10,000. This type of website is suitable for small businesses who want a bigger online presence.
  • Custom Website: A highly customized website with advanced features tailored to specific business needs. This could include integrations with third-party systems, complex databases, a custom report dashboard, or data visualization charts. Custom websites can cost anywhere from $10,000 to $50,000 or more, depending on complexity.
  • Consulting and Ecommerce: For hourly services our rate is $85. This is for clients who wish to engage us for hourly work. It is also suitable for most cases to support a robust Ecommerce site.
  • Ongoing Costs: In addition to the initial development costs, small businesses should budget for ongoing expenses like domain name renewal, cloud services, updates, SEO, or support services. These costs can range from a few hundred dollars to thousands of dollars per year.
